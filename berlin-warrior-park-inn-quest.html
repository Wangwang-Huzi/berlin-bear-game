Run through long Berlin-inspired side-scrolling routes, fight strange graffiti monsters, collect exactly three Park Inn boosts per level, and defeat landmark guardians at the finish.

📘 Controls

How to Play

Each level is a full route before the boss. Survive gaps, platforms, traps, moving hazards, falling objects, graffiti monsters, and the final guardian arena.

⬅️➡️A / Left Arrow and D / Right Arrow move the bear. The camera follows through a long map.
⬆️W, Space, or Up Arrow jumps. Use platforms to cross gaps and avoid traps.
⭐J shoots suitcase stars. Use them to defeat graffiti wall monsters and bosses.
💨K dashes. Dash is vital for timing, knockback recovery, and dodging warning attacks.
🏨Each level has exactly three Park Inn logo boosts. Each restores one third of max HP once.
🎨Graffiti monsters patrol, jump, chase, and shoot paint blobs. Defeating them makes a paint splash.
